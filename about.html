Our mission

Overture is a diverse team of content creators working to create cool shit. In everything we do, we strive for diversity, equity and inclusion by creating spaces for everyone to thrive. We play hard, we fight hard, and we support one another.

Through our various tournaments and events, we have raised over $25,000 for various non-profits around the world. We are also proud to have been nominated in the 2023 Gayming Awards for "Best LGBTQ+ Contribution to Esports".
